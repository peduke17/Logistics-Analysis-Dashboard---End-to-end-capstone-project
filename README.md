# Logistics-Analysis-Dashboard--End-to-end-capstone-project

## Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of logistics operations, fuel management, driver performance, and route profitability.

The dashboard is designed to help transportation and logistics companies monitor operational efficiency, identify cost saving opportunities, improve delivery performance, and optimize route planning.

---

## Business Objectives

- Monitor fleet fuel consumption and costs
- Track fuel efficiency (MPG) trends
- Analyze driver and delivery performance
- Identify top fuel consuming trucks and states
- Evaluate route profitability
- Support operational and strategic decision making

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Fuel Analysis Overview

Key metrics:
- Total Fuel Cost
- Total Gallons Consumed
- Average Fuel Price
- Fuel Efficiency (MPG)
- Total Trucks
- Maintenance Cost

Insights:
- Top states by fuel spend
- Top trucks by fuel cost
- Fuel spend trend by year
- Fuel efficiency trend
- Geographic fuel spending analysis

---

### 2. Driver & Delivery Performance

Key metrics:
- Delivery On-Time Rate
- Driver On-Time Rate
- Fleet Average MPG
- Average Idle Hours
- ⁠Driver on time rate
- Total Drivers

Insights:
- Revenue generated by drivers
- Facility detention analysis
- On-time performance trends
- On time rate by event
- Top performing drivers

---

### 3. Route Profitability Analysis

Key metrics:
- Total Revenue
- Revenue Per Mile
- Total Loads
- Total Routes
- Average Route Distance

Insights:
- Most profitable routes per mile
- Top route by revenue
- Route revenue analysis by origin and destination
- Booking type analysis
- Yearly route performance trends

---

---

## Key Insights

- Fuel represents the largest operational expense.
- Improving fleet MPG can significantly reduce annual fuel costs.
- Certain states contribute disproportionately to fuel spending.
- Driver on-time performance directly impacts delivery success.
- Route profitability varies significantly across locations and route distances.
